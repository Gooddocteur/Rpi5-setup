# Setting up an NVME SSD Drive on Raspberry Pi 5

<details>
<strong>Date</strong>: 11-20-2025 | <strong>Created by</strong>: Julian Gonzalez | <strong>Version</strong>:1.0 | <strong>License</strong>: Apache 2.0 |
</details>

This tutorial aims to teach the basics of adding an NVME SSD drive hat to a Raspberry Pi 5 (8gb) device.

## Materials Used
<div style="text-align: center;">
  <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Jewelzufo/Rpi5-setup/main/Materials.jpg"

The following materials were used for this tutorial:
- 512gb NVME SSD
- Armor Lite V5 Active Cooler (fan)
- Third party NVME hat
- Raspberry Pi 5 (8gb)
- Screwdriver
